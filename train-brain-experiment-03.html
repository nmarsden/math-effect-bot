Train Brain Experiment #3

Train the brain to select a player unit that exists

Using 81 inputs with range 0-1 and 1 output in the range 0-7

  • 81 inputs in the range 0-1 to represent the number of player units
  • 1 output in the range 0-7
  • keep training until average reward is > 0.9
eg. For an input of 1,0,0,.....0
  • reward +1 to the brain for an output in the range 0-3
  • reward -2 to the brain for an output in the range 4-7
